Fight with your Buster sword Keyboard !

When @Dathink has released The DaftPunk Console, I found it was a very cool concept. So I decided to make my own console, with the sounds of Final Fantasy VII.

With your keyboard, cast some cult magics like Bolt 3, or trigger Limit Break after a dash ! I hope FF Fans will enjoy it (and others won't have too much headache ^_^ ).
